ABOUT

This is for the second time MAKAUT, WB students are hosting ANWESHAN one of West Bengal's biggest festivals with variety of entertaining events in both technical and cultural genre, where more than 10,000+ people across all 200+ affiliated colleges will compete with one another to show who is the best with their outstanding performances. Anweshan began as only a concept and eventually expanded with the help of contributor and volunteers. Our primary goal is to make Anweshan 2k23 the best and largest festival in West Bengal. We simply hope to leave a legacy for our juniors in upcoming year.
